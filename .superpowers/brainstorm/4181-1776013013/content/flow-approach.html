How should the Scout screen guide users?

Both keep your existing trait groups — this is about how the page communicates the workflow to first-timers.

A

Guided Step Flow

The screen visually breaks into numbered steps with a persistent progress indicator: Step 1 — Scout Traits, Step 2 — Set Run/Pass Bias, Step 3 — Build. Each step is visually distinct. The Build button is locked until Step 1 is done, with a clear "why" message.

STEP 1 Select opponent traits
STEP 2 Set run/pass tendency
STEP 3 Build your game plan →
Best for: Public release — removes all ambiguity about what to do next

Enhanced Free-Form + Smart Header

Keep the current free-form layout but add a prominent hero instruction block at the top — a short 2-line explainer ("Tag your opponent's tendencies, then build your defensive game plan") with a visual flow arrow. Trait groups get subtle step numbers. The Build CTA becomes more visually dominant as selections are made.
